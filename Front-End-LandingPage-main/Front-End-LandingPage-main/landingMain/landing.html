About Us

Welcome to ScholarU, a pioneering capstone project proudly developed by the dedicated students of the College of Computer Studies at Tarlac State University.

What is ScholarU?

ScholarU is a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the scholarship application process for students at Tarlac State University. Our goal is to make it easier for you to find, apply for, and manage scholarships, all in one convenient place. With ScholarU, you can explore various scholarship opportunities, submit applications, and track your progress seamlessly, ensuring you have the best chance to access the financial support you need for your education – plus an eligibility checker that can check if you meet all of the requirements, and what else you need for programs you clicked!
